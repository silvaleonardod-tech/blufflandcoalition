Get Involved

Whether you're a landowner, volunteer, organization, or neighbor who loves these bluffs — there is a meaningful place for you in the Bluffland Coalition's work.

How You Can Help

Three Ways to Make a Difference

🌿

Volunteer

Join trail-building crews and habitat restoration workdays organized by ORA Trails, Mississippi Valley Conservancy, and coalition members. No experience needed — just boots, gloves, and enthusiasm. Workdays happen year-round across the blufflands.

Sign Up at ORA Trails
🤝

Partner

Organizations and municipalities are welcome to join the coalition by signing the Memorandum of Understanding. We welcome new members who share our commitment to conservation, recreation, and community wellbeing across the region.

Contact Us to Learn More
💚

Donate

Financial support through our member organizations helps fund land acquisition, trail construction, habitat restoration, and community engagement. Every contribution directly protects and enhances the blufflands for future generations.

Give to MV Conservancy
