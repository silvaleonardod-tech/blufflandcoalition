Our Mission

Conserving the
Blufflands that
Define Our Region

The Bluffland Coalition will establish an exceptional network of contiguous protected lands and recreation trails throughout the La Crosse–La Crescent region. Our cooperative efforts enhance the health of residents, visitors, natural ecosystems, and our local economy.

Created in response to the 2016 Blufflands Plan, the Coalition's goals are improved public health, ecological diversity, connected communities, economic strength, and a high quality of life for all who call this region home.

Signature Route

The Bluffland Traverse

A point-to-point route running from the northern end of La Crosse County to the southern end, following the Mississippi River and the bluffs that overlook it.

The route utilizes a combination of road, gravel bike path, shared-use trail, and single-track on public property — connecting the county and its communities. ORA Trails is actively working with coalition members to acquire easements that will bring more of the route into the woods and away from traffic.
