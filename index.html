Our Mission

Conserving the Blufflands that Define Our Region

The Bluffland Coalition will establish an exceptional network of contiguous protected lands and recreation trails throughout the La Crosse–La Crescent region. Our cooperative efforts enhance the health of residents, visitors, natural ecosystems, and our local economy.

Created in response to the 2016 Blufflands Plan, our primary goals are to protect land near urban areas from development, preserve natural spaces for habitat restoration, and create an accessible space for public recreation.

Signature Route

The BlufflandTraverse

A point-to-point route running from the northern end of La Crosse County to the southern end, following the Mississippi River and the bluffs that overlook it.

The route connects communities using a combination of single-track, shared-use trail, gravel bike path, and road on public property. ORA Trails is actively working with coalition members to acquire easements that will bring more of the route into the woods.
